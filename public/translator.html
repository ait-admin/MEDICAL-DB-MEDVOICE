Communication Process Flow

Step-by-step translation workflow

1
Click on "Offline"
Start by selecting the doctor availability status as In-house.
2
Click on "Translator"
Choose the Translator function to begin the translation workflow.
3
Select Doctor/Patient Language
Pick the languages for both doctor and patient from the dropdowns.
4
Press "Doctor Speaks" or "Patient"
Click the respective button to record the message.
5
Click on Speaker to Speak Out
Play the translated message aloud if needed.
6
Click "New Meet" to Clear & Download
Reset the session and download the conversation if required.
